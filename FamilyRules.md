# Family Rules

1. Throw your trash in a trash can or recycle bin.
   1. Throw away food scraps _only_ in the kitchen trash can.
   1. When your bedroom trash can is full, tie up the bag and throw it in the trash bin in the garage.
      Replace the trash back in your bedroom.
   1. If you fill the trash can in a common area, empty the can to the garage trash bin and replace the trash bag.
1. Clear your dishes after eating.
   1. Dump food scraps, except liquids such as soup, into the kitchen trash bin.
   1. Rinse food residue off the dishes.
   1. Place dishwasher-safe dishes in the dishwaser.
      Leave non-dishwasher-safe dishes in the sink.
1. Keep your bedroom clean.
   1. If the Roomba cannot vacuum your room, you will have to vacuum it manually.
1. Keep common areas clean.
   1. When you are done doing something in a common area, put away anything you had out such as toys, pencil & eraser, etc.
   1. Put dirty laundry in your hamper.
1. Recreational screen time is limited to 30 minutes per day.
   This does not include lessons, homework, or texting friends.
   1. You may listen to music while doing homework if it does not disturb others.
      You may _not_ watch videos while doing homework.
1. When you get home from school, camp, etc., immediately change into clean clothes.
   Put your dirty clothese in your hamper.
